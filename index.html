About Me

Who I am and what I do

I am a full stack developer based in Charlotte NC with a foundation in audio engineering, where precision, structure, and problem solving shaped the way I work. I bring that same discipline into software, creating clean interfaces, intentional design, and user experiences that feel effortless.
